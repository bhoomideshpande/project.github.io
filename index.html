City Introduction

Our team has taken the lead in developing a 1500 square kilometer smart city named Chikmanglur which is located on the foothills of the Mullayangiri peak of the Western Ghats with a total population of 1.13 million. With a budget of 6400 crore, we plan to build a city that incorporates digital technologies and data-driven strategies to enhance urban life, optimize resource use, and improve services across infrastructure, transportation, energy, and governance.
